About Me

Hi, I'm Nishant Kumar, a passionate Computer Science undergraduate at KIIT, Bhubaneswar, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I love turning ideas into real, impactful digital solutions—from building interactive web apps to designing and deploying end-to-end machine learning solutions, particularly in Generative AI and LLM orchestration. My work blends creativity with logic, and I enjoy every part of the development process—from writing clean code to solving real-world problems.

Recently, as an AI/ML Intern at Kokku Proptech, I had the opportunity to build a project from the ground up by engineering a complete data-to-model pipeline, fine-tuning a TinyLlama-1.1B model with LoRA, and deploying a full-stack, scalable chatbot. This professional experience complements my personal projects. I developed:

RESUMENTOR, an AI-powered career assistant that automates resume analysis and custom cover letter generation using LangChain and the Google Gemini API. I also created

GreenGLOW, a plant care and disease detection platform that uses a CNN model trained on over 87,000 images to diagnose plant diseases with 97% accuracy. To strengthen my grasp of fundamentals, I also built

Sortify, a web-based sorting algorithm visualizer that helps users understand algorithm behavior through real-time animations.

I'm constantly learning and expanding my toolkit, which includes Python, C++, and Java, alongside frameworks like TensorFlow, Flask, LangChain, LangGraph, and Transformers. I'm particularly focused on advanced techniques like Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), LLM Agent Orchestration, and building Multi-Agent Systems. I'm also a strong believer in teamwork, having led creative initiatives as the Artist of the KIIT Art Society.

Thanks for stopping by—I'm excited to keep growing, building, and collaborating!
