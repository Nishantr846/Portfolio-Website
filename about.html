About Me

Hi, I'm Nishant Kumar, a passionate software engineer currently pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science at KIIT, Bhubaneswar. I love turning ideas into real, impactful digital solutions—whether it's building interactive web apps or training machine learning models. My work blends creativity with logic, and I enjoy every part of the development process—from writing clean code to solving real-world problems.

One of my proudest projects is GreenGLOW, a plant care and disease detection platform that uses a CNN model trained on over 87,000 images to diagnose plant diseases with 97% accuracy. I also built Sortify, a web-based sorting algorithm visualizer that helps users understand algorithm behavior through real-time animations. These projects reflect my passion for both front-end interactivity and back-end intelligence.

I'm constantly learning, whether it's exploring new technologies, improving my DSA skills, or picking up tools like TensorFlow, Flask, or Streamlit. I'm also a strong believer in teamwork, having led creative initiatives as the Artist of the KIIT Art Society.

Thanks for stopping by—I'm excited to keep growing, building, and collaborating!
